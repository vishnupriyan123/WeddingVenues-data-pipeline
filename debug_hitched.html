Your Wedding Venue Planning Guide to London and Beyond

London is a prime spot for any wedding. Its historical buildings, major landmarks, and gastronomic offerings make London a hotspot for luxury wedding venues! Large wedding venues that can cater to 200 or even 500 guests are also abundant in the area. But if you prefer something small and intimate, you'll also find more secluded spaces as well. Whatever your wedding style and budget you are sure to find the right wedding location to tie the knot.

Low-budget weddings or small celebrations also have ample options in London. Most of the venues in London offer wedding packages that will save you money or you can go the DIY route and bring your own suppliers. You can find the best option that meets your budget needs. If you want to save on your wedding venue be sure to check the most affordable wedding venues London has to offer.

If you want a wedding venue in London with outdoor space you can book a stately manor. You'll also find ample wedding venues in London with gardens for your spring or summer nuptials. Remember you can also hire a marquee or tipi to have a plan B in the event of unwanted rain or wind.

If you want a glam city wedding you can choose a gorgeous restaurant near the Trafalgar Square, or dance the night away with views of Big Ben in the distance. Central London has many luxury venues, but you can also narrow your search to South West London wedding venues or East Central London

.

5.0 (1) Bar, Pub and Restaurant London
Katie
5.0 out of 5 rating
5.0

If you're hunting for the ultimate restaurant and private events spot in London, stop scrolling and start booking - Sams Caf in Primrose Hill is an absolute showstopper. I recently hosted a dinner party in their charming, book-lined basement (think cosy literary salon meets secret supper club), and let me tell you - it was like something out of a dream. Big shoutout to Deji, the Manager Extraordinaire, who deserves a knighthood for his patience alone. I threw every tiny request at him - right down to can we nudge the napkin a few inches to the left? - and he met each one with grace, clarity, and a genuinely warm smile. He stayed late to walk through my increasingly detailed vision, never once said no, and somehow made me feel like I was the only event that mattered. Plus, every visit came with complimentary coffee or drinks (the kind of hospitality thats becoming all too rare in London!). The food? Delicious. The drinks? Perfectly poured. The service? Impeccable. The vibe? Straight-up magical. And the location? Primrose Hill, wow. It doesnt get more gorgeous than that. Deji also worked with us to keep everything within a very fair budget without compromising on experience - which only makes me want to come back and host a dozen more parties. In short: if you're planning a special dinner, private party, or just want an excellent meal in a beautiful space, Sams Caf is THE ONE. Dont even think twice. Highly, highly recommend. Thank you Deji and team. Katie
